Clients may request a therapy report for various reasons, such as for understanding their child's progress, submitting insurance claims, communicating with other professionals, or fulfilling administrative requirements. A therapy report typically includes the reason for referral, background information, assessments, session observations, therapy progress, and recommendations.

Generally, it will take 2 weeks to process a therapy report, from the time the request is submitted.
