Parent Consultation

Many parents may not fully understand the effectiveness of counselling or creative arts and play therapy in addressing their children's emotional or behavioral challenges. To provide clarity, we offer parent consultation to new clients who wish to understand the therapy process before committing to it for their children. Additionally, the therapist will assess the needs and background of your child before commencing therapy.

Throughout the therapy process, parents often seek updates on their child's progress, Thus, they are encouraged to request parent consultation for this purpose. The therapist can also provide parents with tools to meet their child's emotional needs.

Parent consultation sessions can be conducted via Zoom for convenience.
