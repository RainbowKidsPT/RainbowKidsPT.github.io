Safe Management Measures

  1. If the parent or child is unwell, the parent should reschedule the appointment to another day.
  2. The child must wash his/her hands before and after the play therapy session.
  3. Toys and furniture will be sanitized after each visit. There are 45 minutes in between each session arranged for sanitization.
  4. The therapist will continue to wear a mask during the therapy session.
