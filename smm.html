Safe Management Measures

  1. Parent must check in and check out of therapy office for himself/ herself and the child using SafeEntry to help MOH in establishing potential transmission chains.
  2. Body temperature of all visitors will be measured before entering the therapy office.
  3. Only one parent is allowed to bring the child for therapy.
  4. Parent, child and therapist must wear a mask at all times during therapy.
  5. If parent or child is unwell, parent should reschedule the appointment to another day.
  6. Therapist will keep at least 1 metre apart from the child wherever possible in the therapy room.
  7. Parent must wash/sanitize his/her hands after entering the therapy office. Child must wash his/her hands before and after the play therapy session.
  8. Toys and furniture will be sanitized after each visit. There are 45 minutes in between each session arranged for sanitization.
