Clinical Supervision

As a registered counsellor and a certified play therapist, I decided to support the younger generation of play therapists through clinical supervision. Though I am still a trainee clinical supervisor, I bring a rich blend of experience working with both children and adults.

My journey into play therapy deepened my appreciation for the unique language of children, while my background in counselling provides a solid foundation for supporting professionals across developmental stages.

I offer a reflective, collaborative and creative supervision space where play therapists can explore their clinical work, deepen their skills, and grow with confidence and integrity in their therapeutic practice.
