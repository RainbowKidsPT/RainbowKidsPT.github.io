Training

Jeanie has conducted training for social workers and counsellors in using creative arts and play therapy to work with children in a social service agency.

She is now conducting training courses for Orego for social workers, school counsellors, teachers, and parents so that they can understand how creative arts and play therapy can support children/teenagers with emotional and behavioural issues.
